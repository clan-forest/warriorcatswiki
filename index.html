Clan Ranks

Leader

Leads the Clan and usually receives nine lives from StarClan.

Deputy

Second-in-command. Organizes patrols and becomes leader when needed.

Medicine Cat

Heals injuries, treats sickness, and communicates with StarClan.

Warrior

Protects the Clan, hunts prey, trains apprentices, and follows the warrior code.

Apprentice

A young cat training to become a warrior or medicine cat.

Elder

A retired Clan cat respected for wisdom, stories, and experience.

The Prophecies Begin

A new generation of cats faces danger and the Clans must search for a new home.

Centers around three young cats connected to a mysterious prophecy.

Dawn of the Clans

Explores how the original warrior Clans began.
