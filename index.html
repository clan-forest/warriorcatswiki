Warrior Cats Info

A fan-made guide to Clans, ranks, characters, and books

Clans Ranks Characters Books Terms

The Clans

ThunderClan

ThunderClan cats live in the forest and are known for bravery, loyalty, and strong warrior hearts. They are often seen as noble and protective.

RiverClan

RiverClan cats live near water. They are excellent swimmers and are skilled at catching fish. They are proud and graceful.

WindClan

WindClan cats live on the moor. They are fast runners, slim, and tough. They are known for catching rabbits.

ShadowClan

ShadowClan cats are clever, secretive, and strong. They often live in darker, marshy areas and are sometimes misunderstood by other Clans.

SkyClan

SkyClan cats are great jumpers and climbers. They were separated from the other Clans for a long time before returning.

StarClan

StarClan is where many warrior ancestors go after death. They guide the living cats through dreams, signs, and prophecies.

The Dark Forest

The Dark Forest is where some evil or dishonorable cats go after death. It is a dangerous place full of shadows and fear.

Clan Ranks

Leader

The leader controls the Clan and makes major decisions. Leaders usually receive nine lives from StarClan.

Deputy

The deputy is second-in-command. They organize patrols and become leader if the current leader dies or retires.

Medicine Cat

The medicine cat heals injuries and sickness. They also speak with StarClan and interpret signs.

Warrior

Warriors protect the Clan, hunt for food, train apprentices, and follow the warrior code.

Apprentice

Apprentices are young cats training to become warriors or medicine cats. Their names usually end in “paw.”

Kit

Kits are baby cats. Their names usually end in “kit.” They stay in the nursery until they become apprentices.

Elder

Elders are retired Clan cats. They are respected for their wisdom and stories.

Important Characters

Firestar

Firestar began as a kittypet named Rusty. He joined ThunderClan, became Firepaw, then Fireheart, and eventually Firestar.

Bluestar

Bluestar was the ThunderClan leader who allowed Rusty to join the Clan. She was wise, powerful, and tragic.

Graystripe

Graystripe is Firestar’s best friend. He is funny, loyal, and sometimes struggles between love and loyalty.

Ravenpaw

Ravenpaw was a ThunderClan apprentice who knew dangerous secrets. He later found a safer life away from the Clans.

Tigerstar

Tigerstar is one of the most famous villains in Warrior Cats. He was ambitious, dangerous, and power-hungry.

Sandstorm

Sandstorm started out doubting Firestar, but later became one of his closest companions and a strong warrior.

Yellowfang

Yellowfang was a grumpy but caring medicine cat. She became very important to ThunderClan.

Book Arcs

The Prophecies Begin

The first arc follows Rusty as he joins ThunderClan and becomes Firestar. It introduces the Clans, warrior code, and major conflicts.

The New Prophecy

This arc follows a new generation of cats as the Clans face danger and must find a new home.

Power of Three

This arc focuses on three young cats with a mysterious prophecy surrounding them.

Omen of the Stars

This arc continues the prophecy storyline and includes a major battle involving the Dark Forest.

Dawn of the Clans

This arc goes back in time and explains how the warrior Clans first began.

A Vision of Shadows

This arc introduces new challenges for the Clans and brings SkyClan back into the main story.

Warrior Cats Terms

Kittypet

A house cat that lives with humans.

Twolegs

What Clan cats call humans.

Fresh-kill

Food hunted by Clan cats, like mice, birds, fish, or rabbits.

Gathering

A meeting where all Clans come together during a full moon.

Warrior Code

The rules Clan cats are expected to follow. It teaches loyalty, courage, and respect for the Clans.

Moonstone / Moonpool

A sacred place where medicine cats and leaders communicate with StarClan.
